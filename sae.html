Formula SAE

Work on UConn Formula SAE, contributing to design and subsystem collaboration on a student-built race car.

I have contributed to both Aerodynamics and IC Powertrain subsystems. My work includes CAD modeling, packaging, and supporting design decisions that affect performance and manufacturability. Being part of FSAE has taught me how design, testing, and collaboration all tie together on a complex system.

  • CAD work on subsystem components and assemblies.
  • Discussion and coordination with other subsystems.
  • Focus on practical, buildable solutions.
